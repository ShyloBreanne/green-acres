Green Acres Farm Sanctuary
Nonprofit organization with multiple programs to help farm animals

Meet Dahlia:
She has permanent brain damage and will require bottle feedings for the rest of her life. Green Acres rescued Dahlia and gives her extra love and attention.

"The mission of Green Acres Farm Sanctuary is the prevention of cruelty to, and the commercialization of animals, especially farmed animals. We put this mission into action by providing shelter, care and board to abused, abandoned, and unwanted farm animals. We look to inspire change through creating a place where members of the public can have positive interaction with farm animals by providing for their care and the animals can, in turn, educate them about the abusive use of farmed animals." -greenacresfarmsanctuary.org
